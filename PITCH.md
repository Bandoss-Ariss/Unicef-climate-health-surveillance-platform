# 🌍 UNICEF Climate-Health Platform — 1-Minute Pitch

---

## 🚨 The Problem

Burkina Faso is facing a major humanitarian crisis characterized by insecurity, population displacement, and recurrent climate shocks. This situation affects approximately **4.4 million people**, including **2.3 million children**, who have multisectoral needs.

Three critical challenges have been identified:

1. **Lack of anticipation** — Crises hit before health systems can prepare
2. **Inefficient early warning** — Existing alert systems are fragmented and slow
3. **Frequent service interruptions** — Power outages break vaccine cold chains, cut water supply, and halt emergency care

**The result:** preventable child deaths from dehydration, malnutrition, and untreated malaria — because health centers go dark when children need them most.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

An **integrated open-source platform** combining two innovations:

### 1. Smart Solar Health Facilities
- Solar systems with IoT sensors monitoring **energy, water, and cold chain** in real time
- Automatic alerts when battery drops, cold chain breaks, or water runs out
- Predictive maintenance to prevent failures before they happen

### 2. Climate-Health Early Warning System
- **AI models** that predict disease outbreaks 7–14 days in advance (malaria, malnutrition, dehydration)
- **Climate risk assessment** combining satellite data, weather forecasts, and health indicators
- **Multilingual prevention messages** (French, Mooré, Dioula, Fulfuldé) sent via SMS to mothers and health workers — automatically triggered by climate thresholds

### 3. Crisis Simulation Engine
- Monte Carlo simulations to model "what if" scenarios (heat wave, flood, epidemic)
- Cascade effect analysis: energy failure → cold chain break → vaccine loss → child deaths
- Generates **action plans and resource estimates** before the crisis hits

---

## 🤖 AI & Innovation

| Component | Technology | Impact |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Epidemic Predictor | LSTM Neural Network | Forecasts outbreaks 14 days ahead (F1: 0.83) |
| Energy Forecaster | XGBoost + LSTM Hybrid | Predicts solar failures before they happen (R²: 0.92) |
| Climate Risk Model | Random Forest + Satellite Data | Multi-hazard risk assessment (AUC: 0.91) |
| Translation Engine | mBART Transformer | Health messages in 4 local languages |
